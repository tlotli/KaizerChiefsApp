    Katsande: We must support Komphela

    Amakhosi will host Ajax Cape Town at the FNB Stadium on Saturday evening – the side that handed Komphela the first of many disappointments in his debut season when they lost to the Urban Warriors 1-0 in the MTN8 final.

    Katsande, who joined his current employers from the Capetonion outfit, feels the Glamour Boys have actually had a good season but have been unlucky in the vital stages of the campaign. “They (Ajax) now have a very different team from the one I played for,” he told the club’s official website. “I don’t think we have been as bad as many people perceive. The problem is that we have failed to turn our dominance in more goals.” With 10 draws this season, the lack of consistent goal-scoring within the squad has seen the Soweto giants fail to defend the Premiership title, but the Zimbabwean feels Komphela is not to blame. “He deserves our full support. We just need an element of luck to show the work that we put in to prepare for the games,” he added.
